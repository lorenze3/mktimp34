Measuring Marketing Impact

Try it now!

No Touch Marketing Measurement

Marketing measurement needs to be seamless and low effort for full integration into the Planning-Execution-Optimization cycle. This app is a prototype for what that looks like

Real Marketing Mix Models

This app runs real marketing mix models, identifying the best model from a range of technical indicators and heuristics. It then applies the champion model to decompose the target variable and produces a simple, interactive dashboard.

This is Just a Start

To be a complete marketing measurement tool the app needs a few things:

  • More Power -- graduating from a single instance on free tier Azure gives the app enough compute to try many more models on larger data.
  • ROIs -- Integrating costs into the dashboard KPIs
  • Response Functions -- To feed optimizations producing profit maximizing allocations

Technologies

Built in Flask, deployed to Azure as a webapp with an intergrated MySQL. Python's scikit learn is the modeling engine; Plotly is used for visualization
