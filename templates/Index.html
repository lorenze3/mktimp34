Measuring Marketing Impact

Try it now!

No Touch Marketing Measurement

Marketing measurement needs to be seamless and low effort for full integration into the Planning-Execution-Optimization cycle. This app does that.

Real Marketing Mix Models

These are real marketing mix models. The app identifies the best model from a range of technical indicators and heuristics and then applies the champion model to decompose the target variable and produces a simple, interactive dashboard.

Just a Prototype

To be a complete marketing measurement tool the app needs a few things:

  • More Power -- graduating from a single instance on free tier Azure would give the app enough compute to try many more models on larger data.
  • ROIs -- to ensure marketing spend is efficient, financial data needs to be incorporated into the reporting
  • Response Functions -- to enable optimization of marketing drivers

Technologies

Built in Flask, deployed to Azure as a webapp with an intergrated MySQL. Python's scikit learn is the modeling engine; Plotly is used for visualization
